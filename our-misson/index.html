Our Mission

Our mission is to establish circular economy. We believe that the world we live in is dying and that we need to protect it. To help us, we encourage you as the consumers to give us your soakers if they need repairing. We also encourage you to give us old sheets of fabric with a minimum dimension of 50cm x 40cm and we can make it into a pair of soakers.
